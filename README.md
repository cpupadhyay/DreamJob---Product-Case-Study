DreamJob – Fresher Job Marketplace Case Study

- DreamJob is a marketplace prototype designed to address inefficiencies in entry-level job discovery for 0–2 year candidates.

  Problem: 

- Freshers face high competition from experienced candidates on general job platforms, resulting in low interview conversion rates.

 Solution: 

- A verticalized fresher-exclusive hiring ecosystem with:

- Skill-based candidate scoring

- Recruiter-side filtering

- Application tracking

- Resume optimization

Key Metrics:

- North Star: Interview Conversion Rate

- Marketplace health metrics defined

-Cold start risk analysis included

Status:

- Research and prototype stage (Figma).
- No live MVP deployed.
